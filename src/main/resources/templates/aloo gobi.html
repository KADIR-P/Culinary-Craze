Let's make Aloo Gobi

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup of potato cubes
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of spices
    1. Garam masala
    2. Turmeric powder
    3. Ginger garlic paste
    4. Coriander powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  • Remove the leaves from the cauliflower and separate each floret. Put the cauliflower florets in warm water for 5 minutes. After 5 minutes once again wash the florets in cold water and drain the water completely.
  • Peel the potato skin and cube them. Put it in a bowl of water.
  • Heat 3 to 4 tbsp oil in a pressure cooker or pan. Add the cumins. (This sabji requires some good amount of oil).
  • Allow the cumins to pop and then add the green chillies.
  • Next, add the potato cubes. Cook the potatoes, stirring it frequently for a few minutes.
  • After 4 to 5 minutes add the cauliflower florets. Stir them well.
  • Next, add the remaining ingredients - turmeric powder, chilly powder and required salt. Stir everything well.
  • Stir everything well. (No need to add any water)
  • Reduce the heat to low and close it with the pressure cooker lid. (For these type of sabjis choose a small 2 ltr pressure cooker or pan).
  • After 2 minutes put the whistle. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes and turn off the heat.
  • Open the cooker after 5 to 7 minutes. Add the chopped coriander leaves and transfer to the serving dish.
  • Serve it hot with roti, pulav or nann.
