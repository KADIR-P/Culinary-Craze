Let's make Chana Masala

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of canned chickpeas
  • 1 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  • Rinse and soak white chana for 5 - 6 hours.
  • Next take oil in a pressure cooker and heat it. Add in 1/2 tsp jeera and 1 bay leaf.
  • When the cumin seed splutters, add in giner+garic paste. Give a quick stir.
  • Next add in chopped onion and fry until soft.
  • Next add in chopped tomato and fry until soft.
  • Add in dry spice powders like red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, amchur powder and garam masala powder. Give a quick mix.
  • Next add in salt and turmeric powder.
  • Add in soaked white chickpeas.
  • Close the lid and allow it to cook until white chickpeas are soft.
  • Once chickpeas or chana is cooked, mash some chick peas using back of a spatula.
  • Boil it again for few minutes.
  • Switch off the stove and add in batter.
  • Serve it with roti or chapathi.
