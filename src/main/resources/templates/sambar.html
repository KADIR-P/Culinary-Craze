Let's make Sambar

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup of Sooji/Semolina
  • 2 Cups of water
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1/2 inch ginger (grated)
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1/4 cup carrots (diced)
  • 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves

Steps:

  • Wash and soak tur dal in 1 cup of water for 10 mins.
  • Soak tamarind in warm water for 10 mins , then extract the juice and discard pulp.
  • Chop onions to big pieces , slit green chillies.
  • Add tur dal along with water to a pressure cooker , chopped onions , curry leaves , cumin ,turmeric powder , sambar powder ,chilli powder , tomato , tamarind water , 1 1/2 cup water , salt ,jaggery and close the pan.
  • Cook for 5 whistles.
  • Once pressure subsides open the cooker and mash the sambar well with your ladle.
  • In a small pan heat ghee , add mustard and when mustard splutters add it to boiled sambar and mix well.
  • Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
